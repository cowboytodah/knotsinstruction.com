Fishing Knots

Fishing knots are the most searched knot category online. The right knot can mean the difference between landing a fish and losing it — these instructions cover every essential terminal tackle and line-to-line connection.

Advertisement

Palomar Knot

Easy

Strongest fishing knot — retains nearly 100% line strength. Best for hooks, lures, and swivels on mono and braid.

Improved Clinch Knot

Easy

World's most used fishing knot. Five wraps plus a back-through step. Excellent for monofilament and fluorocarbon.

Uni Knot

Easy

The universal knot — works on every line type including braid. Also joins two lines as the Double Uni.

Blood Knot

Advanced

Classic fly fishing line-to-line join for two similar diameter tippet sections. Compact barrel passes cleanly through guides.

Surgeon's Knot

Easy

Fastest reliable way to join lines of different diameters. Two overhand wraps — ideal for tippet to leader connections.

Albright Knot

Intermediate

Connects very different diameter lines — light leader to heavy braid or mono to wire. Slim profile passes through guides.

Nail Knot

Intermediate

Standard fly line to leader connection. Ultra-slim join that lies flat against the fly line and passes through guides cleanly.

Loop to Loop

Easy

Quick-change leader connection using two interlocking loops. Disconnect easily for fast leader swaps on the water.

Surgeon's Loop

Easy

Strong permanent loop at the end of any line for loop-to-loop connections and leader attachment.
