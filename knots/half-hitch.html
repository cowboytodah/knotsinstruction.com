How to Tie a Half Hitch

Easy
⛵ Sailing⛺ Camping🏕️ General

The half hitch is a simple securing hitch used to finish many other knots — two half hitches is one of the most reliable general-purpose fastening methods in sailing and camping.

Written Instructions — Half Hitch

  1. Wrap around post Pass rope around post and bring working end back alongside standing part.
  2. Form loop around standing part Make a loop in the working end going around the standing part.
  3. Tuck through loop Pass working end through the loop from front to back. Pull snug.
  4. Add second half hitch Repeat for a second half hitch below the first — two half hitches is the standard reliable fastening.

Tips for Tying a Half Hitch

  • One half hitch alone will slip — always use two half hitches or finish with a half hitch after another knot.
  • A round turn followed by two half hitches is one of the most reliable all-purpose hitches in seamanship.
  • Both half hitches must go in the same direction — reversed half hitches form a half-clove hitch instead.
  • After tying, give the load end a sharp tug to test — if either hitch shifts, retie.
