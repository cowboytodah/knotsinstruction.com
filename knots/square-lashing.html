Written Instructions — Square Lashing

  1. Start with clove hitch Tie a clove hitch on the vertical pole right at the crossing point.
  2. Diagonal wraps Wrap rope diagonally: over vertical, under horizontal, 3–4 times, keeping tight.
  3. Frapping turns Wrap 2–3 times between the poles in the gap at the joint to cinch wraps tight.
  4. Finish with clove hitch End with a clove hitch on the horizontal pole right at the joint. Pull everything tight.

Tips for Tying a Square Lashing

  • The frapping turns are what make the lashing rigid — skipping them leaves a floppy joint.
  • Each wrap should be as tight as you can pull it — strength comes from friction, not from the knot itself.
  • Start and finish on the same pole for a neater appearance and easier untying.
  • A properly tied square lashing supports the weight of a standing person on a structure — the standard of excellence.
